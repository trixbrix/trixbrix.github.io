Update your TrixBrix devices directly from your browser. Plug in via USB, click install, done.

Unsupported browser

Firmware flashing requires the WebSerial API, which is only available in Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera). Please open this page in one of those to continue.
