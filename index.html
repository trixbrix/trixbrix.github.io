Browser-based firmware updater

Snap. Plug. Flash.

No installer, no Python, no fuss. Open this page in Chrome or Edge, plug your Trixbrix device in over USB, and click one button to install the latest firmware.

Heads up — this browser can't flash devices

USB flashing needs the WebSerial API, which only works in Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera). Reopen this page in one of those to continue.
